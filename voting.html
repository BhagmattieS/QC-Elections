HOW TO VOTE

------------

In order to vote, first you must open the form below which will be open only on the day of elections during the recess periods. Prior to the day of elections, you will be presented with the candidates contesting for the presidential position of the student council. On the day of elections, this form will be accessible to all students and it will be mandatory for every student to vote. In the form, you will be provided with a mulitple choice option to select the candidate of your desire.
